About us

Our lab's vision is to expand the borders of the game medium. With a focus on innovation and experimentation, LIVE is a research space aimed at creating experiences that go beyond actual game mechanics, aesthetics and man-machine interfaces. We believe that the unique combination of engineering values with a multidisciplinary environment creates a fundamental polymathy to advance the state of the art. This allows generating greater training and visibility opportunities for developers in response to a competitive industry.
LIVE also seeks in a constant and avant-garde way to reflect critically on its development processes to facilitate the creation of tomorrow's videogames.
